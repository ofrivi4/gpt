Description

Enjoy the best of both worlds at Chadwick Manor. A quiet and tranquil duplex apartment within a historic building in the countryside, but only 10 minutes’ drive from the shops of Knowle and trains at Dorridge, Lapworth and Warwick Parkway to take you onwards to Birmingham or London. Birmingham airport is 15 minutes away and motorway access in 7 minutes. The apartment is perfect to move straight into. The light and spacious living room has glorious views over the shared gardens and the countryside beyond, with a fitted fireplace and radiator covers. The ample dining kitchen has an electric double oven range cooker and integrated dishwasher and washer / dryer which are available by separate agreement. There is an upstairs WC and cloakroom. The main double bedroom has 5 fitted wardrobes, an en-suite bathroom and access to the patio, which is perfect for lazy sunny afternoons. The second double bedroom has a built in desk and cupboards, making it perfect for a home office or craft room. The adjacent bathroom room with WC, along with a stretch of fitted wardrobes, make this an ideal space for guests. The property benefits from recently installed fibre broadband with the current owner having speeds up to 120 Mbps. The apartment has two separate garages and there is plenty of communal parking for when friends and relatives visit, along with a shared tennis court. The quiet grounds are regularly visited by deer, foxes, badgers and squirrels.
