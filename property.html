Further Details:

This property would sit well between the pages of any Homes and Garden Magazine. Every conceivable aspect of multi generational family living has been thought through. The design offers versatile accommodation for modern family living and to the ground floor includes three large reception rooms and a magnificent open plan kitchen / dining and family room with full height glass to the rear elevation taking full advantage of the outstanding garden views. First floor does not disappoint with five bedrooms and two en-suites a family bathroom and plenty of storage. The décor is modern allowing a new owner to literally drop their bags and start living. The house is underfloor heated with gas. Flooring is engineered light oak adding the spacious feel to ground floor. Location Cheswick Way is a semi-rural area with a vibrant local community so popular with young families. Nearby Shirley is enriched with leisure and retail facilities. The new Parkgate Shopping Development is a vibrant community area accommodating Asda, restaurants, shops and a gymnasium. Shirley High Street also offers additional independent retail outlets and restaurants to suit all tastes and cultures. Within easy access there is a wide range of superstores including Sainsbury's, Tesco, Aldi, Marks & Spencer and a large retail park housing furniture, electrical and DIY retailers. For family education there are a good choice of both Primary and Secondary schools to suit all requirements. Commuters have easy access to the M42 & M40 motorways and there are regular bus and train links to Solihull, Birmingham and Stratford Upon Avon.
